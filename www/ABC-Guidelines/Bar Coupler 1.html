CONNECTION DETAIL
COMPONENTS CONNECTED
  • Precast concrete shell with SCC core
  • Precast footing
DESCRIPTION
  • A template is used to align longitudinal bars in the footing and column.
  • Transition bars measuring approximately 12 bar diameters are manufactured to connection bars in the footing and column by upset headed couplers, which are torqued as specified by the manufacturer.
  • Before the erection of precast column, a spiral is placed around the bars protruding from the footing, and raised and tied around the longitudinal bars after installation of transition bars.
  • The connection region is eventually formed and filled by pumping a cementitious grout.

UTILIZING
  • validated by experimental test
ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
DESIGN RECOMENDATIONS
  • Testing observation showed that slipping would occur within upset headed couplers under large lateral loads.
ADVANTAGES CHALLENGE
• Both column and foundation can be precast and connected. • The connection is cumbersome to construct, and require tight bar length tolerance.
