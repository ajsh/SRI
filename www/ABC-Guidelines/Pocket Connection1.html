CONNECTION DETAIL
COMPONENTS CONNECTED
  • Precast column
  • Precast bent cap
DESCRIPTION
  • A single steel pip in the bent cap is used to house the bars extended from column and serve as a stay-in-place form as well as equivalent joint hoop reinforcement.
  • After placing precast bent cap, concrete is placed in the pocket and bedding layer.

UTILIZING
  • validated by experimental test
ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
DESIGN RECOMENDATIONS
  • The thickness of pipe should be designed to provide the same circumferential hoop force in the joint as that required by transverse reinforcement of Article 8.13.3 of the 2009 LRFD SGS.
    • ADVANTAGES CHALLENGE
    Both column and bent cap can be precast and sufficiently connected. Inappropriate concrete fill in pipe would reduce the performance of connection.
    The connection allows relatively large tolerance for location of steel pipe and extended column bars.
