CONNECTION DETAIL
COMPONENTS CONNECTED
  • Precast column
  • Precast bent cap
DESCRIPTION
  • Two taper pockets are reserved in precast bent cap, and a small amount of longitudinal reinforcing bars are extended out from top of column.
  • After erecting the precast bent cap on the top of column, grout is poured to closure the pocket and connect the precast bent cap with column.

UTILIZING
  • validated by experimental test
ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
DESIGN RECOMENDATIONS

Multiple anchorage methods (straight bar, headed bar, hooked bar, etc.) would be considered to develop column reinforcing bars in pocket connection.

ADVANTAGES CHALLENGE
Grout can be poured into the taper pocket rather than pumped to a narrow avoid. Moment resistance is primarily developed in longitudinal direction of bent cap.
