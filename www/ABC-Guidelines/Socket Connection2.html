CONNECTION DETAIL
COMPONENTS CONNECTED
  • Concrete filled steel tube
  • Precast pile cap
DESCRIPTION
  • •The steel tube can set into void on precast foundation and attached with light bolts.
  • • High strength fiber-reinforced grout is filled between the tube and the corrugated pipe.
  • •The steel tube is filled with low shrinkage self-consolidating concrete to achieve concrete filled steel tube (CFT).

UTILIZING
  • validated by experimental test
ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
DESIGN RECOMENDATIONS

A diameter-to-steel tube thickness ratio, D/t, of 80, which exceeds limiting ratio of 62.5 recommended by AISC 2005 provisions, was tested to be sufficient.
An embedment depth of 0.75D was sufficient to develop the full composite resistance of the CFT member.

ADVANTAGES CHALLENGE
CFT provides better confinement of the concrete fill. Curing time is required for concrete fill to gain strength before the subsequent construction.
No formwork is needed in field. Corrosion of steel tube is a potential long term issue.
