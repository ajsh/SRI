CONNECTION DETAIL
COMPONENTS CONNECTED
  • Precast column
  • Cast-in-place (CIP) footing
DESCRIPTION
  • • The precast column with intentional roughening on the surface embedded in foundation is firstly positioned and braced.
  • • After the foundation reinforcement is placed around the column, the foundation is cast.

UTILIZING
  • validated by experimental test
  • used in practical project
ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
DESIGN RECOMENDATIONS

An embedment depth of 1.1D was sufficient to develop the plastic hinge of column.
The bottom diagonal bars proved unnecessary by experimentally tests and can be eliminated.

ADVANTAGES CHALLENGE
It avoids any potential problem of fit-up of bars in sleeves or ducts and grouting.
