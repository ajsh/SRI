His primary research focus is in the area of large scale testbeds, which has led to the creation of The Internet-Scale Event and Attack Generation Environment (ISEAGE-pronounced "ice age").  This facility creates a virtual Internet that allows researchers and vendors to test in a "real-life Internet environment" various ways of attacking and various ways/systems of protecting information...

He is also leading an effort to build a state wide cyber range to support training, and cyber exercises.  This project is a joint effort between the State of Iowa, Iowa National Guard, ISU, and the ICN.
 
He working with the state of Iowa, Iowa National Guard, Iowa Homeland Security, FBI, and others to develop the State of Iowa Cyber plan, and to help develop programs and test-beds to increase the security of Iowa and to help in cyber security preparedness.
